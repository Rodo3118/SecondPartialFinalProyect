Ingredients:
4 cups of tomato juice
1/3 cup lemon juice (4 to 6 lemons, depending on size)
5 tablespoons hot sauce
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 cup of ice
1 beer, light or dark

Preparation of the michelada:
1.-In a bowl or container, mix tomato juice, lemon, salsa, Worcestershire sauce, and soy sauce.
2.-Frost a cold jar or glass with salt, or chili powder, to taste.
3.-Add 1 cup of ice to your jar or glass, and 1/2 cup of the michelada mix.
4.-Pour the beer into the mug or glass, mix with a spoon, and voila! 
5.-Now you can enjoy your michelada.

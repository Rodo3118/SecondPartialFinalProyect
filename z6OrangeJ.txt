Ingredients:
Fresh orange juice
3 cups of water
1/3 cup of sugar
Ice cubes
Citrus press, else you can use your hands

Process:
1.-Wash the oranges, cut them in half, and squeeze the juice from each orange with a citrus press (or just by squeezing the juice with your hands).
2.-Pour the cup of juice into a pitcher. Add the 3 cups of water, 1/3 cup of sugar and stir.
3.-Taste to see if fresh orange water needs more sugar (some oranges are sweeter than others).
4.-Finally, add the ice cubes and serve. If you want to decorate your drink, add a couple of fresh mint leaves. 
5.-Enjoy your Orange Water

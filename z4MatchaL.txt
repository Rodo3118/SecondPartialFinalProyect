Ingredients:
1 tablespoon of matcha
1/4 cup very hot water
2 tsp of honey
1/2 cup of milk

Preparation:
1.-In a wide cup place the matcha. 
2.-Add the tablespoon of water and whisk vigorously until dissolved.
3.-Add the rest of the hot water and mix again.
4.-In a saucepan, heat the milk and dissolve the honey in it. 
5.-Mix the milk vigorously until light foam forms and pour into the cup with the matcha.
6.-As easy as that, you have your matcha, the only thing left is to enjoy!

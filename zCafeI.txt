Ingredients:
1 glass of whiskey (preferably Irish)
1/2 liter of coffee
200 grams of liquid cream (milk cream)
40 grams of sugar

Preparation Method:
1.-Heat the whiskey with sugar and wait until it boils.
2.-Divide the mixture into 4 glasses.
3.-Pour hot coffee on top. Have it ready in advance.
4.-Assemble the milk cream. Once ready, you can put it in a pastry bag or use it like this.
5.-Cover the glasses with the cream. Enjoy this drink without stirring, so the cream will remain on the surface

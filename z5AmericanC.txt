Ingredients:
7g fine ground coffee
1 cup of hot water, preferably high-quality (not tap water)
Machine for Coffee

Process:
1.-Fill the coffee load in your coffee pot attachment. Gently knock it on the table and assemble it.
2.-Whether you have the process on automatic or you control the time, count 25 seconds of dripping into the cup and stop the machine.
3.-Once you have your shot of espresso, pour it on a Cup for coffee
4.-Fill it up with boiling water
5.-Enjoy your American Coffee!

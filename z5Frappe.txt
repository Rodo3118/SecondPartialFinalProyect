INGREDIENTS:
1 cup of cold milk
4 tablespoons of coffee syrup (water, coffee and a little sugar)
2 teaspoons syrup or chocolate sauce
1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
Ground cinnamon for sprinkling, optional
2 cups of ice

PROCESS:
1.-The first thing you have to do is place all the ingredients (except the cinnamon and ice) in a blender, process everything for about 10 seconds at medium speed.
2.-If you have an ice crusher, it will be time to crush it and, once you have it, pour it into the bottom of two tall glasses. 
3.-Over these glasses, also pour the frappe coffee that we have just prepared.
4.-And you are ready to drink your Frappe, Enjoy!

Ingredients: 
45 ml of white rum
35 ml of lime juice
15 ml of fruit juice, such as strawberry, pineapple, banana, watermelon, or peach
crushed ice
Shaker or blender

Preparation:
1.-Place all ingredients in a shaker or blender. 
2.-You can add pieces of fruit to give it more thickness, although they are usually strained at the end.
3.-Finally, serve in a martini glass and decorate the rim with sugar to make it a sweeter and more tropical drink.

Ingredients:
4 cups of pineapple
2 tablespoons sugar or to taste
1/4 cup hot water
4 cups of water

Process of creating:
For the syrup, mix the hot or boiling water with the sugar until it has dissolved.
Peel and cut the pineapple removing the hard center of the pineapple. Cut into large squares and place in the blender along with 2 cups of water.
Blend very well and pour into a jar. 
Then add the remaining 2 cups of water and the simple sugar syrup to sweeten.
Pour it in a glass with ice, and its ready, Enjoy!
